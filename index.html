Pick a card to see the eeveelution!

    • Dusk stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      It is as dark as dark can be. Going into a dungeon and using this stone makes the eevee evolve into a Umbreon.

    • Dawn stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      An odd stone that glints like an eye. Having only 2 applications this stone can only evolve an eevee depending on gender.

    • Fire stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      The stone has a fiery orange heart. This stone has 3 applications making it easy to use. Either a dungeon, a luminous cave or spring is needed.

    • Thunder stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      It has a distinct thunderbolt pattern. Using this on an eevee evolves it to a jolteon if the requirement is met of being in the Luminous cave or spring

    • Leaf stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      Having an unmistakable leaf pattern. This stone is easily recognizable. By simply touching a pokemon in a dungeon the pokemon evolves.

    • Shiny stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      Shining with dazzling light. This stone has to be offered at the Luminous spring to evolve a pokemon.

    • Water stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      As blue as a pool of water. This stone has 3 applications making it easy to use. Either a dungeon, a luminous cave or spring is needed.

    • Sun stone

      EVOLUTION ITEM

      This stone glows with a sunny warmth making it seemingly the same as a fire stone. The only difference being the evolution applications.
